 Add WP Enquiry button before the posts or before the products with many options.

== Installation ==

1. Make sure your WordPress site are installed WooCommerce.
2. Install and activate this plugin.
3. Go to /wp-admin, on WooCommerce menu, click WPEnquiry.
4. Your button is ready.
